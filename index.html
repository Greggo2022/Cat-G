Cat Gregory

Editor

Cat Gregory is an editor best known for her work on the award-winning BBC anthology series Inside No. 9, written by and starring Reece Shearsmith and Steve Pemberton, as well as the CBBC adaptation of Enid Blyton's much-loved series Malory Towers , working with director Rebecca Rycroft for King Bert productions. Cat has recently finished working as editor on the interactive comedy-horror movie House Hack, shot and edited in Tallinn, Estonia with creator/director Stephanie Zari. Previously Cat cut the pilot episodes of fairytale/fantasy anthology Whatever After with writer/director Jack Jewers and one episode of comedy web series Acting Up, which was selected for the Portland Film Festival and was nominated for 3 awards at the UK Offline Web Awards, with Jordan Baker winning the award for best actress. Prior to this Cat was assembly editor on series 3 of Harlots for Hulu, Capital for the BBC and Spotless for Tandem Communications, as well as Inside No. 9.
